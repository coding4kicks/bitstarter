Bitcoin is the digital currency of the future. It enables individuals to be free in their transactions. Yet the infrastructure underpinning this freedom depends upon the sacrifices of individuals. Help those men and women, who represent in their actions, a dedication to the freedom of others. Help disabled veterans step into a brighter future.

Wounded Warriors is an outstanding charity that supports US military members injured in combat. Bitcoin For Vets acts as a bitcoin payment gateway, with all converted funds going directly to Wounded Warriors. Make a donation and leave a comment for whoever passes by. If you don’t have bitcoin and would like to make a donation directly, follow this link to Wounded Warrior’s donation page.

FAQ

What are the levels: red, white, and blue?
Red is 1$, white is 5$ or more, blue is 10$ or more. Larger donations result in a larger percent of time in the shout out box.
What message can I leave?
Anything you want. Just use good taste.
Why did you build this site?
1) For fun for a Coursera class. 2) An attempt to offer a small bit of help to those who need it.
Who are you?
Poof, a Navy veteran learning to play with computers. I served with VFA-131, SOTF-C, and VMFAT-101. Grab any code from the site on Github.
